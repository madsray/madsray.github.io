about

Hello, my name is Madison Ray and I am a web developer based in Nashville, TN. I am a born and raised Texan (Go Astros!) who moved to Nashville in 2013 to attend Belmont University where I received my BBA in Music Business with a minor in Design Communication. Through internships at Dualtone Music Group, Oprah Magazine, and Thirty Tigers, I have gained experience in graphic design, fashion magazine operations, and project management. Since graduating, I have completed my certification in web development from General Assembly. I am an innovator who loves to tell stories and solve problems for my clients in an easy to use and graphically appealing way.

When not working I am most likely spoiling my maltipoo, Winston, who is my sidekick in all things cuddling and Netflix. I love where I live, Nashville’s music scene and offbeat style has inspired much of my work. It’s also possible that I have a slight candle addiction and a serious love for cactus, must be the Texas in me. I hope you enjoy looking around my website and please contact me if you have any questions or an idea you want to turn into a reality.
